Example YAML:

Top level argument is one of ['bar', 'solid', 'wait', 'with_nested'] With nested must be followed by a list ('-' on each new line, indented) and also contain the key 'list' at the same level of indendation as with_nested (but with no '-'). Bar must have [width, speed, angle, barColor, backgroundColor]. Wait must have [time]. Solid must have [time, backgroundColor]. Grating must have [width, speed, angle, barColor, backgroundColor, wavelength] and time OR numberOfBars.
